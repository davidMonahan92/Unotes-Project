FAQ

What is Unotes?

Unotes is designed as a Web Application, but responsive on all devices. The idea behind the App was to create a learning app for older DCU students. The application allow students full access to their college lecture material where they are able view the notes in multiple ways to provide easy access, they can choose how to access the material as a course overview, week by week, by topic or the full resource material. They can also choose the way they want to view the material, by text, through audio, by video or as a slideshow.

Do You have to be a DCU Student ?

Yes. Only DCU students have access to Unotes and the course specific materials within. You must have a valid DCU email to create an account and log in.

What features does Unotes have ?

Users will have access to their course material in the form of a course overview, week by week, by topic or the full resource material. They can then choose the way they want to view the material, by text, through audio, by video or as a slideshow.

Who is Unotes designed for ?

Unotes is aimed at the older generation of student (Over the age of 60). As students over the age of 60 might not have the same skill and abilities to use technologies as someone in their twenties, the aim is to design and make this application as simple as possible to navigate and appealing to older students making it very accessible. I also want to make this app as accessible as possible including students with disabilities and other diverse user groups. With simple, fluent features this app will allow anyone of any skill type and range to use it.
